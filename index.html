The Problem

Natural disasters often leave survivors trapped in areas where traditional search efforts are dangerous and slow. Limited visibility due to smoke, dust, or darkness makes detection even harder. Our drone aims to solve this by providing rapid aerial reconnaissance and automated survivor detection.

Objective

Deploy an autonomous UAV that integrates thermal imaging, RGB vision, AI-based detection, and geotagging to locate survivors quickly and transmit precise coordinates to rescue teams.

Key Technologies

MLX90640 Thermal Imaging – Detects heat signatures even in smoke or darkness.
ESP32-CAM RGB Vision – Captures visual details for identification.
GNSS Geotagging – Provides exact survivor locations on maps.
Onboard AI – Runs YOLO/CNN models to detect humans in real time.
Obstacle Avoidance – Ensures safe navigation in rough terrains.
450 mm Quadcopter – Lightweight, modular design for easy deployment.

How It Works

Detection & Fusion

  • Thermal and RGB camera streams are synchronized.
  • AI identifies human figures from combined data.
  • Detections are tagged with GPS coordinates.

Communication

  • Drone transmits alerts and location to rescuers.
  • Mission control receives live data and mapping.
  • Operators can monitor from a safe distance.

Impact & Benefits

  • Rapid reconnaissance in disaster-hit areas.
  • Enhanced visibility in low-light conditions.
  • Safe access to dangerous or collapsed zones.
  • Cost-effective compared to helicopters.
  • Protects human rescuers from risk.
  • Speeds up life-saving operations.

Future Enhancements
